How To Make A Salad


1. grab apples, lattuce, spinash, cucumbers and other "green foods".


2. wash those green foods with water and perhaps soap depending where you got them from.


3. put those foods on a cutting board.


4. cut said foods into peices.


6. put the cut peices into a bowl.


7. add a tad bit of salt or tajin. some lemon too.


8. done.
